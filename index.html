What is Kava?

Kava 卡瓦胡椒 (Piper methysticum) is consumed for medicinal, religious, political, cultural and social purposes throughout the islands of the South Pacific.
A shrub that thrives in loose, well-drained soils where plenty of air can reach the roots, kava plants are typically harvested at 3-4 years of age. Pacific Islanders have consumed kava root tea for over 3,000 years. We invite you to experience the tradition for yourself.
As time has progressed so have the techniques used to make this sacred island drink. We want you to be able to experience the best kava available. At Kava Kings we strive to make the highest quality kava available to the kava community and consumers all over the world.
